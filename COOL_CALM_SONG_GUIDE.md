## 🎚️ PRODUCTION ARRANGEMENT GUIDE

### **Tempo Selection (For Cool/Calm Vibe)**
- **Use 148-155 BPM tracks** (slower, more relaxed)
- Recommended loops: **12, 13, 14, 15, 11, 18, 19**
- Avoid 165-170 BPM for main composition (save for transitions/builds)

---

### **SECTION-BY-SECTION BREAKDOWN**

#### **Intro/Build (0:00 - 0:30)**
- **Track 13 or 14** (148 BPM) - Clean electronic intro, minimal
- Add light reverb + subtle filter automation
- Volume: 40-50% (sparse, moody)

#### **Verse 1 (0:30 - 1:15)** 
- **Track 13** (148 BPM) - Main baseline
- **Track 19** (155 BPM) - add atmospheric pad layer at 0:45
- **Track 15** (148 BPM) - Add subtle melodic element at 1:00
- Volume progression: Build from 50% → 70%

#### **Pre-Chorus Build (1:15 - 1:50)**
- Keep Track 13 + 19 + 15 active
- **Track 18** (155 BPM) - Add driving beat pattern
- **Stack 2-3 loops** for fuller sound
- Volume: 75% (building energy)

#### **Chorus Peak (1:50 - 2:35)**
- **Track 11** (155 BPM) - Full orchestration begins
- **Track 12** (150 BPM) - Add bass-heavy layer
- Layer 4 tracks together: 13, 19, 18, 12
- High-pass filter sweeps on Track 19 for dynamic movement
- Volume: 85-90% (PEAK)

#### **Verse 2 Comedown (2:35 - 3:05)**
- Remove Track 12 (reduce bass)
- Keep Tracks 13, 19, 18 
- **Track 14** (148 BPM) - Reintroduce minimal textures
- Volume: Back down to 70%
- Add reverb tails and ambient color

#### **Final Chorus & Outro (3:05 - 3:32)**
- **Bring back all 4-5 layers** for climax
- Use delay effect on vocals/melody line
- Gradual volume reduction from 85% → 0% (27 seconds fade-out)
- Last 5 seconds: Deep reverb tail + pad fade

## 🎛️ MIXING TIPS FOR YOUR TRACKS

### **EQ Guidelines:**
- **Track 13/14/15** (Cool vibes): Light high-pass filter, keep mids warm
- **Track 18/19** (Edge): Boost 2-4kHz for definition
- **Track 11/12** (Bass layers): Subtractive EQ - remove mud below 80Hz

### **Effects to Apply:**
- **Reverb**: Medium-large space (2-4 second decay)
- **Delay**: 1/4 note syncopated (adds space without muddiness)
- **Compression**: Light (1.5:1 ratio) on master track for cohesion
- **Automation**: Slowly sweep filters upward during builds

### **Volume Structure:**
```
Intro (30%)  →  Verse 1 (50%)  →  Pre-Chorus (70%)  →  
Chorus (85%)  →  Verse 2 (70%)  →  Final Chorus (90%)  →  
Outro Fade (0%)
```
